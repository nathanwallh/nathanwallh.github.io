About me

Hi! I am Nathan Wallheimer, PhD student in theoretical computer science at the Weizmann Institute of Science, fortunately advised by Prof. Amir Abboud. My main research area is complexity theory, and specifically fine-grained complexity. Other interests are extremal and additive combinatorics and their applications in algorithms. Previously, I did my masters at the University of Haifa under the supervision of Prof. Oren Weimann, where we worked on planar graphs algorithms. You can contact me via the institutional email appearing in the latest publication of mine.
