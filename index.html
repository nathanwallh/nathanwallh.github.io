About me

Hi! I am Nathan Wallheimer, a third-year PhD student in Theoretical Computer Science at the Weizmann Institute of Science, advised by Prof. Amir Abboud. My main research area is complexity theory, specifically fine-grained complexity. I am also interested in extremal and additive combinatorics and their applications in algorithms.

Previously, I completed my Master's at the University of Haifa under the supervision of Prof. Oren Weimann, where we worked on algorithms for planar graphs. You can contact me via nathan.wallheimer@weizmann.ac.il.
